Feed the Hungry Nepal MUN

Feed the Hungry Nepal Model United Nations is a charity event targeting youths of different age to represent different countries. All the proceeds of this event will go directly to Feed the Hungry Nepal. By participating in this event not only are you exploring and polishing your capabilities but also helping to feed a family.
