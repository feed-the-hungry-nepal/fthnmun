Feed the Hungry Nepal has been working effortlessly since the past 4 months to help the helpless. Through ration distributions and Project Thaali, they have been feeding the people in need. Like the cause itself, the areas to work on, widened. Hence, FTHNMUN2020 came alive. Model United Nations (MUNs) play a key role in changing and shaping lives. If given attention and participation, MUNs are a great means of honing your capabilities. Involving youths and giving them a space to openly discuss their ideas and challenge perspectives is what we believe empowers them. Model United Nations is an effective way to explore your capabilities and polish your skills while also developing them. Through logical discussions and diplomatic dealings, an MUNer stands out from their comfort zone. Through this MUN we also want to bring awareness among the youths on subjects such as hunger eradication. FTHNMUN is a charity event targeting youths of different age to represent different countries. All the proceeds of this event will go directly to Feed the Hungry Nepal. By participating in this event not only are you exploring and polishing your capabilities but also helping to feed a family. This festive season, help the helpless.



Secretary General's Note



Dear Sponsors, Delegates, and Friends of HIMUNC,

On behalf of Henrico High School Model United Nations, it is my honor to invite you to the fourth annual Henrico Invitational Model United Nations Conference. This year HIMUNC IV will be virtual and held on January 22-23, 2021. The conference will be held using a combination of two secure platforms, Zoom and Wonder (Previously Known as Yotribe). Wonder is a platform that will be used for unmoderated caucuses that allows delegates to move freely and create their own blocs.

Model UN has the ability to let students speak up for what they believe in and become involved in the world. It cultivates students to grow into future leaders who are knowledgeable, confident, driven, and passionate to make positive changes in the lives of others. It is not an overstatement to say that MUN has shaped me into the person I am today, and I hope it molds other delegates to be determined, strong, and confident as well.

HIMUNC IV is founded on introducing delegates to major world problems, developing the next diplomats, and inspiring a generation of young minds to be the change they want to see in the world, all from their home. Whether it be finding solutions to ensure the progress of the Sustainable Development Goals, saving the College Board organization, or debating on how to protect humankind from furious Kaiju monsters, delegates will be engaged in challenging debate that encompasses the true essence of MUN.

Even virtually, I assure you that we will still offer the same nurturing and challenging environment that allows our delegates to grow into the leaders of tomorrow. The secretariat and I are working tirelessly to ensure that this HIMUNC will be one of the best yet. Please visit our COVID-19 page for more information on the virtual conference and payment.

The theme of HIMUNC IV is to rise above the storm- the belief that you can overcome obstacles if you rise to the challenge by taking action and standing up for what you believe in. In the past several months, we have faced raging wildfires, a worldwide pandemic, racial injustice, and much more. It is my hope that delegates will have the courage to rise above the storm whether it’s overcoming an obstacle in committee or the real world. With this hope, delegates will rise above to build a better world for tomorrow. Please feel free to contact me or any other secretariat member with any questions or concerns. Registration is now open, and I am looking forward to seeing your delegation at HIMUNC IV!

Sincerely,

Sreya Peddi

Secretary-General of HIMUNC IV
